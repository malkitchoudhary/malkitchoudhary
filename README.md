## About Me

I'm a final-year BCA student at **Manipal University Jaipur**, currently deepening my expertise through a specialized **Data Science & Machine Learning training program at Tips-G (RNS IT Solution)**. I don't just follow tutorials — I build complete ML pipelines from raw, messy data to deployment-ready predictions, tackling everything from data leakage bugs to feature fusion with `scipy.sparse.hstack`.

My work spans **NLP-driven recommendation systems**, **gradient-boosted classifiers (LightGBM, XGBoost)**, and **content-based filtering engines**, all trained and validated through active Kaggle competition. I care as much about *why* a model works as I do about the metric it produces — which is why I dig into gate mathematics of LSTMs, debug pipelines line-by-line, and stress-test my understanding through 300+ self-authored interview questions covering statistics, feature selection, and MLOps.

Right now, I'm expanding into **multimodal AI and Transformer architectures**, building toward systems that reason across text, structured data, and beyond.
